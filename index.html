Project 2 - Experimenter

It is an Android app that simplifies experiment and test recording with built-in patterns for Binomial and Non-Negative tests, and displays locations through Google Map API.

The app allows in-depth analysis of experiment data. Overall, this app is a valuable tool to efficiently conduct and analyze experiments on the go.
